Flood Preparedness Tips

  • Before Flood: Prepare an emergency kit, plan evacuation routes, and store important documents in waterproof containers.
  • During Flood: Avoid walking or driving through flood waters, and move to higher ground if safe to do so.
  • After Flood: Avoid flood-affected areas, disinfect items that were in contact with floodwater, and listen for emergency information.
  • Stay Informed: Monitor weather alerts and updates from local authorities.
